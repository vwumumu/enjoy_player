Final Selection

Stress-testing Variant 2 (Glowing Core) and Variant 4 (Shaded Facets) across different color treatments to see how versatile they are.

Concept A: Glowing Core

Enjoy Blue

The standard brand color. The screen blend mode creates a vibrant, digital energy at the center.

Vibrant Gradient

Adding a purple hue to the gradient enhances the "glowing" effect, making it feel very premium.

Monochrome (White)

Proves the geometry works perfectly even without color. Great for watermarks or minimalist UI headers.

Concept B: Shaded Facets

Enjoy Blue

The standard brand color. The dark borders give it a crisp, origami-like 3D structure.

Vibrant Gradient

The gradient flows across the facets, enhancing the illusion of light hitting a 3D object.

Monochrome (White)

Using white opacities creates a sleek, frosted glass aesthetic that looks incredibly sharp.
